About INet Lab

Foster promising research ideas. Deliver solutions that actually run.

The Intelligent Computer Networks Research Group focuses on Internet of Things (IoT) and Internet of Vehicles (IoV) environments — with particular emphasis on developing reliable, sustainable communication, computing, and resource management.

◎

Mission

Foster promising research ideas and deliver innovative, applicable solutions for IoT and IoV communication, with a focus on assessing and maintaining link health across heterogeneous infrastructures.

◑

Vision

Networks that enable intelligent decision-making at the edge — where data-driven learning and semantic representations combine to improve awareness, adaptability, and robustness.

◐

Objectives

Address centralized and decentralized architectures; ensure efficient, reliable communication delivery and resource allocation under dynamic, resource-constrained conditions.

Our approach

Intelligence that fits inside the constraint, not around it.

A core objective of the group is enabling intelligent decision-making at the network edge, by deploying models that operate effectively within constrained environments. We integrate data-driven learning with semantic representations to enhance network awareness, adaptability, and robustness across IoT applications.

Lightweight ML Compact language models Semantic reasoning Edge deployment Network digital twins

Working principle

Model the network as a graph, reason over it with the right architecture for the job, then act — locally, at the edge, in real time.
